Loco Moco Recipe

Serves 4
Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef chuck
  • 1 cup sliced onion
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 12oz jar of brown gravy
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 cups cooked rice
Instructions:
  1. Heat large skillet with cooking spray over medium heat
  2. Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and form into patties
  3. Fry patties in skillet approximately 6 minutes per side. Center should read at 140 degrees F
  4. Remove patties while leaving oil and patty drippings in skillet
  5. Reduce skillet heat to low and stir in onion and water into skillet until onions slightly softened
  6. Stir in mushrooms and cook for approximately 5 minutes more
  7. Pour gravy over mixture in skillet until gravy is hot
  8. Lay patties in gravy until reheated trhough
  9. While patties simmer in gravy, fry eggs in separate skillet until white is opaque but the yolk remains runny
  10. Divide rice between 4 plates and top each rice portion with a patty, egg, and gravy
  11. Serve and eat!
